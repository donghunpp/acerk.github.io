Tennis Network, Match and Ranking System Project

Problem:

Finding suitable tennis partners and booking courts during peak hours is frustrating. Court owners also suffer from revenue loss due to static pricing models.

Solution:

AceRk is a mobile app that opens the tennis network, enables competitive matchmaking, and maximizes court usage through a smart auction-based reservation system.

Key Features:

• Community building through network connections

• Tennis partner matching (ranked or casual)

• Court reservation with dynamic pricing

• Player ranking and analytics dashboard

Business Model:

• % commission from court bookings

• Player subscription (premium filters, data analytics)

• SaaS for court owners (auction dashboard, analytics)

Target Market:

• Urban tennis players (25-45)

• Tennis clubs and independent court operators
