Rank System

Visualize the competitive ranking hierarchy using a pyramid structure. Tiers are based on cumulative percentile and distribution ratio:

• Diamond: Top 4% (Cumulative 4%)
• Platinum: Next 7% (Cumulative 11%)
• Gold: Next 12% (Cumulative 23%)
• Silver: Next 47% (Cumulative 70%)
• Bronze: Bottom 30% (Cumulative 100%)

This system motivates players with a competitive mindset, promotes player engagement, and contributes to expanding the tennis ecosystem.
